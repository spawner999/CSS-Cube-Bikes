Reaction GTC SL

When you leave the rest behind on the GTC SL, it'll be clear why. There's simply no competition for this neon green or black-and-grey racer, whether in 27.5" or 29" wheel options. In either case, the GTC carbon frame with high performance, lightweight components - a complete XT group and new Fox Float 32 suspension fork - is the ideal bike for cross-country, marathon racing or just epic day rides.

SPECIFICS

FRAME GTC Twin Mold Monocoque Technology, ARG2, Tapered Headtube, X12, Integrated Cable Routing
SIZE 27.5: 16", 18" // 29er: 15", 17", 19", 21", 23"
FORK Fox 32 Float FIT4, 15QR, Tapered, Remote Lockout, 100mm
SHIFTERS Shimano XT SL-M8000-I, Direct Attach
BRAKE SYSTEM Shimano XT BR-M8000, Hydr. Disc Brake (180/160)
